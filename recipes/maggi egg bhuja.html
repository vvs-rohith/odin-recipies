Lemon Egg Bhurji MAGGI

I used to make this recipe when I was living in my hostel during my uni 1st and 2nd years
This recipe is super easy to make and is delicious
Hope you enjoy it

Ingredients Required

  1. MAGGI 2-Minute Noodles,1 packet
  2. Onion(chopped),1
  3. tomato(chopped),1
  4. dried red chillies,3
  5. eggs,2 beaten 1 omelette
  6. curry leaves,10
  7. salt
  8. oil

Procedure

  1. Heat the oil and sauté the onions until they are soft and light brown. Add tomato, red chillies and curry leaves. Sauté, till the tomato has softened.
  2. Add the beaten eggs and mix well, using a fork to break the eggs into small pieces as they cook (like scrambled eggs). Add salt to taste.
  3. Cook the MAGGI Masala Noodles as per pack instructions and add to the Egg Bhurji preparation. Mix well.
  4. Serve hot.Garnish with omelette
