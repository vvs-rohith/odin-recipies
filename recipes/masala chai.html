Stuffed Eggplant

one of my favourite drinks
very easy best consumed HOT
Hope you enjoy preparing and drinking it

Ingredients Required

  1. 40 grams cardamom
  2. 20 grams cloves
  3. 20 grams pepper
  4. 8 grams cinnamon
  5. 10 grams fennel
  6. 30 grams dry ginger
  7. 6 tulsi seeds
  8. ½ nutmeg
  9. 2 cup water
  10. 4 tsp tea powder
  11. 1 cup milk
  12. 2 tsp sugar

Procedure

  1. firstly, in a heavy-bottomed pan take 40 grams cardamom, 20 grams cloves, 20 grams pepper, 8 grams cinnamon and 10 grams fennel.
  2. dry roast on low flame until it turns aromatic. keep aside.
  3. now take 30 grams dry ginger and break into pieces.
  4. dry roast until it turns aromatic. keep aside.
  5. further add 6 tulsi seeds, ½ nutmeg and roast well.
  6. cool completely, and transfer all the spice mix into blender.
  7. blend to a slightly coarse powder. chai masala powder is ready, you can store in an airtight container and use for a month.
  8. firstly, in a saucepan take 2 cup water, 4 tsp tea powder and ½ tsp chai masala.
  9. boil for 4 minutes or until strong concoction is prepared.
  10. now add 1 cup milk, 2 tsp sugar and boil the chai twice.
  11. strain the masala tea and enjoy.
