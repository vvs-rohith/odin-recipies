Stuffed Eggplant

one of my favourite recipe
This recipe is a little complex but trust me it is worth it
Hope you enjoy making and eating it

Ingredients Required

  1. 12 Small eggplants / Eggplants
  2. 1-2 Slit Green Chillies
  3. 2-3 tbsp Oil
  4. 1-2 tbsp Coriander Leaves

    5. For Stuffing

  5. 1 Dry Red Chilli
  6. 2 Small Onions
  7. ½ cup Peanuts
  8. 1 tbsp Coriander Seeds
  9. 2 tbsp Desiccated Coconut / Dry Coconut
  10. 1 inch Cinnamon Stick
  11. 3-4 Cloves
  12. 1 inch Ginger
  13. 3 Garlic Cloves
  14. 1-2 Green Chillies
  15. ¼ tsp Turmeric Powder
  16. Tamarind small lemon size ball
  17. 1 tsp Jaggery
  18. 1 tbsp + 1 tsp Oil
  19. Salt to taste

Procedure

  1. To prepare the stuffing, heat a tsp of oil in a pan and add cloves, coriander seeds, cinnamon stick and peanut and fry until golden brown color.
  2. Add dry red chilli and fry for a couple of seconds.
  3. Take them out of the pan and keep it aside.
  4. In the same pan, add one tablespoon of oil and add and sauté slit green chillies and chopped onions until they are transparent.
  5. Add dry coconut or desiccated coconut to it and give it a mix.
  6. Combine the roasted peanuts mixture, sautéed onion, turmeric, ginger, garlic, tamarind, salt and jaggery in a blender and grind to a smooth paste by adding a little water. Keep it aside.
  7. Wash the eggplants and make a plus (+) slit on the bottom of the eggplant. Keep one end intact.
  8. Now stuff the eggplants with the ground mixture and keep them aside.
  9. Heat 2-3 tablespoons of oil in a pan. Place the stuffed eggplants carefully into the pan and give it a mix.
  10. Add the leftover filling and one cup of water to the pan.
  11. Mix well and cover with a lid.
  12. Let it cook over a medium - flame until they are soft and cooked.
  13. Keep stirring in between and add more water if needed. Adjust salt if needed.
  14. Once they are done, add chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
